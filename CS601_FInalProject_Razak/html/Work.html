Test Engineering

As a test engineer I am in the business of writing procedures based on customer specs to validate customer requirements. These procedures are executed by skilled technicians. Writing procedures that are indicative of real world application requires forethought and a detail orientated mind. Stepwise instructions requires the writer to imagine the obstacles an operator may face and try to make performing the task as easy as possible. Performing the test is a collaborative effort that involves my oversight and their skilled labor. Test data is collected using data acquisition systems and presented to internal inpsection.

Work Style
