Fitness

Life is all about finding balance. Which means you are in a perpetual battle with yourself and your surroundings. if you feel comfortable then you arent balanced but have let one thing over take another. For example stand on your non dominant foot and lift the knee of the other leg to your chest. Notice the twitching in your planted foot. how it struggles to support you as your bodies natural rhythm seeks to topple you one way or the other. That concentration and struggle you feel is balance and its not easy to maintain. But thats balance.
